Screwdriver

Description

This simple, classic cocktail will be sure to whet your whistle. And your guests will think you're quite the bartender.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz squeezed or store-bought orange juice
  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 1.5 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Combine orange juice and vodka in a garnished glass, stir, and add ice.
