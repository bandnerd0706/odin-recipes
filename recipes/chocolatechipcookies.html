Mac and Cheese

Description

In this recipe, we combine our ingredients of cheese, pasta, and milk to create a classic dish: Macaroni and Cheese. We season this dish with some Worcestershire sauce, mustard powder, and onion powder to provide a tangy twist on the classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 1.75 pounds macaroni
  • 0.75 cup butter
  • 0.75 cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 TBS Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 8 oz shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 24 oz shredded American cheese
  • 8 oz potato chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • butter-flavored cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil. Cook macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally, 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Melt butter in large pot over medium-low heat. Slowly mix in flour, whisking constantly; cook until no longer smells like flour and is brown, about 5 min.
  4. Pour 1 cup milk into mixture, whisking thoroughly, about 45 sec. Repeat twice.
  5. Whisk remaining 3 cups milk. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, mustard powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cook about 10 minutes until thick. Add half the shredded CHeddar, stirring until it melts completely. Repeat with remaining CHeddar and American cheese, about 4 ounces at a time. Remove from heat once cheese is fully incorporated.
  7. Stir macaroni into cheese sauce. Divide between two 9x13-inch baking dishes.
  8. Crush and mix potato chips, 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese in a bowl. Top macaroni evenly with potato mixture. Spray with cooking spray.
  9. Bake in oven until crust is golden brown and sauce is bubbling, about 35-45 min.


Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies


Description

This simple recipe for an American tradition, chocolate-chip cookies, promises to take the fuss out of preparation and get you straight into thinking, "Wow...that was easy!"

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 0.5 cup shortening
  • 0.75 cup packed brown sugar
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1.5 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in large bowl.
  3. Beat softened butter, shortening, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in another bowl until creamy. Beat in egg, then gradually add flour mixture and mix well.
  4. Add in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet until edges are starting to turn golden brown, about 8-10 minutes.
